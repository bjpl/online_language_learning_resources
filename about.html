About Language Learning Hub

Language learning resources for 68 languages

What is this?

Language Learning Hub is a collection of language learning resources covering 68 languages. It includes widely-studied languages like Spanish, French, Chinese, and German, as well as indigenous languages like Cree, Navajo, and Quechua. Each language page lists apps, courses, books, audio resources, and practice tools.

What you'll find here

  • Online courses and platforms
  • Mobile apps
  • Books and textbooks
  • Audio and video resources
  • Language exchange and practice tools
